About us

We are two French Engineering students, passionnate about D&D and tabletop in general. We created this website as part of our course "Cloud Computing" at the ESILV Engineering School. We chose to create an interactive webapp, complete with an immersive UI and a connected database. The webapp is designed to provide a library of fantasy-themed item cards for the D&D 5E tabletop. It is aimed at fans of roleplay games and homebrew (fan-made content). We designed it using two dockerized systems, one for the database that contains the item cards, and the other for the frontend of the project.
